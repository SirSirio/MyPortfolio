    parent is still at `opacity: 0`, then the child "appears" with no motion. It looks like a random
    inconsistency and it will make the draw effect look broken rather than absent.

      the identical edit. The `h2` in each section keeps its own `data-reveal="0"` — it is already a
      sibling of `.section__head`, so it is unaffected.
