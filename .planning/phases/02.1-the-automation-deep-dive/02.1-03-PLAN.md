deep-dive feel like a machine assembling itself. This is the layer of visible wow that
signals "this person builds with AI/advanced tooling" (D-17): parts assemble, the protocol
traces itself, blueprint lines draw on as the visitor scrolls (D-13, MOTION-01). Because
the deep-field engine is paused behind the overlay (D-20), GSAP safely owns the subpage's
frame budget.

he steers by reacting to rendered results (D-03, D-11..D-13). The optional animated OT-2
deck (D-12) is a strictly upside-only throwaway: sketched, then promoted only if it clearly
lands, otherwise dropped with zero risk to the page. The timelapse already carries success
criterion 4 (from Plan 01), so nothing here is load-bearing for "moving parts" — this is
the enrichment on top.

Purpose: turn a media-forward case study into a dynamic one that demonstrates advanced
build skill.
Output: vendored ScrollTrigger, a clean gsap.context lifecycle, and Sirio-approved
scroll-telling on the deep-dive.
